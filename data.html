Balance of Payments/International Investment Position (xlsx)

This workbook contains time series of the major items of the Balance of Payments and International Investment Position of 95 countries, The starting date varies by country. For the annual data, the earliest starting year is 1960, while for the quarterly data the earliest starting date is 1989:Q1. The first sheet, "boo-iip template," maps the conventional presentation of the BOP and the IIP onto variable codes, using Australia as an example. The second sheet, "list of all BOP-IIP variables," lists all the variables in the IMF's BOP/IIP database, with a description. The original source is the IMF's BOP database: http://data.imf.org/BOP. This version: 9/23/2019.

David Jacks' commodity prices: 1850-present

Real prices of 40 commodities, as well as indices. A description of the dataset and an account of the author's findings is provided at Jacks, D.S. (2019), "From Boom to Bust: A Typology of Real Commodity Prices in the Long Run," Cliometrica, 13(2), 202-220.
