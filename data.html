Balance of Payments/International Investment Position (xlsx)

This workbook contains time series of the major items of the Balance of Payments and International Investment Position of 95 countries, The starting date varies by country. For the annual data, the earliest starting year is 1960, while for the quarterly data the earliest starting date is 1989:Q1. The first sheet, "boo-iip template," maps the conventional presentation of the BOP and the IIP onto variable codes, using Australia as an example. The second sheet, "list of all BOP-IIP variables," lists all the variables in the IMF's BOP/IIP database, with a description. The original source is the IMF's BOP database: http://data.imf.org/BOP. This version: 9/23/2019.


External datasets

Real commodity prices

Description: Real prices of 40 commodities, as well as three indices (value-of-production weights at 1975 and 2011 shares, and equal weights), and three sub-indices (commodities to be grown, commodities in the ground, and commodities in the ground, ex-energy). The indices and sub-indices start in 1900. Each individual price series is expressed in U.S. dollars and deflated by the U.S. CPI.

Period: 1850-2018.

Documentation or metadata: David Jacks' site and Jacks, D.S. (2019), "From Boom to Bust: A Typology of Real Commodity Prices in the Long Run," Cliometrica, 13(2), 202-220. (Working paper version at NBER).

Link to data: xlsx


Standardized world inequality database

Description: Gini indices for 196 countries.

Period: Varies by country. The earliest year is 1960. Version 8.1 extends the dataset to 2018 or latest available year.

Documentation or metadata: Frederick Solt's site.

Link to data: Dataset repository at Harvard Dataverse.
