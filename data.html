Balance of Payments/International Investment Position (xlsx)

This workbook contains time series of the major items of the Balance of Payments and International Investment Position of 95 countries, at annual frequency. The starting year varies by country. The first sheet, "boo-iif template," maps the conventional presentation of the BOP and the IIF onto variable codes, using Australia as an example. The second sheet, "list of all BOP-IIF variables," lists all the variables in the IMF's BOP/IIF database, with a variable code and a description. The original source is the IMF's BOP database: http://data.imf.org/BOP. This version: 9/23/2019.

David Jacks' commodity prices: 1850-present

Real prices of 40 commodities, as well as indices. A description of the dataset and an account of David Jacks' findings can be found at Jacks, D.S. (2019), "From Boom to Bust: A Typology of Real Commodity Prices in the Long Run," Cliometrica, 13(2), 202-220.
