Francisco Torralba

I am a research economist based in Chicago, IL, working at Morningstar Investment Management. My fields of expertise are macroeconomics and finance, and I work mostly on the relationship between macro-financial conditions and asset prices. I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Chicago, with a thesis on household consumption and precautionary saving. I am a national of Spain.
